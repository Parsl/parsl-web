First Parsl Community Meeting (ParslFest)

Join us for the first Parsl Community Meeting to be held October 3-4 at the University of Chicago.

The meeting will bring together researchers, developers, and cyberinfrastructure experts from around the country to discuss experiences using Parsl and contribute to developing the Parsl roadmap.

The two day meeting will include presentations and discussions on the first day and tutorials and helpdesk sessions on the second day.

If you would like to participate by giving a presentation, joining the discussion sessions, or attending the tutorial please send us an email.

We have limited travel support available. If you require travel support please send us an email.
