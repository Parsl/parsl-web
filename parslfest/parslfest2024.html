ParslFest 2024 - The Parsl Community Meeting (September 26-27)

The sixth ParslFest Community Meeting was held as a hybrid meeting on September 26-27, 2024. It started around noon on the 26th, with talks that day, and on the morning of the 27th. The afternoon of the 27th was an open hacking session for hands-on work, hacking, testing, bring-your-own-code, etc. The in-person component was held at the University of Chicago.

The meeting brought together researchers, developers, and cyberinfrastructure experts from around the world to discuss experiences using and developing Parsl and Globus Compute (formerly funcX) - a federated function-as-a-service platform related to Parsl.
